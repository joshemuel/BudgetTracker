Budget Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

Budget Tracker is a self-hosted personal finance app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used and stored, and how you can have it removed. It applies to the web app at budgettracker.ddns.net and to any mobile app distributed from the same project.

Who runs this app

The app is operated privately by its owner for a small, invite-only group of users. For any privacy question or request, contact josia.shemuel@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Account information. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and your Google account identifier (a unique ID). If you instead use a username and password, we store the username and a salted hash of the password — never the password itself.
  • Financial data you enter. The transactions, budgets, subscriptions, categories, amounts, dates, currencies, and notes you add to keep your ledger.
  • Optional assistant messages. If you choose to use the Telegram assistant, the text (and any receipt image) you send is processed to create ledger entries.
  • A session cookie. A single secure, http-only cookie keeps you signed in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies and there are no third-party analytics.

How we use it

Your data is used only to provide the budgeting features to you: recording entries, computing totals and projections, and showing your dashboard. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or build profiles from it.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In — used only to authenticate you. Google's handling of your sign-in is governed by Google's own privacy policy.
  • Google Gemini API — only if you use the optional Telegram assistant, the text/image you send is forwarded to Google's Generative Language API to interpret it into a ledger entry.
  • Exchange-rate provider — used to convert between currencies. No personal or financial data is sent; only currency codes are requested.

Where it's stored & how it's protected

Data is stored in a private PostgreSQL database on a self-hosted server. All traffic is encrypted in transit with HTTPS/TLS. Access requires signing in, and new accounts are manually approved by an administrator before they can see any data. Each user sees only their own ledger.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. You can request a copy of your data or permanent deletion of your account and all associated entries at any time by emailing josia.shemuel@gmail.com. Deletion requests are honored promptly.

Children

This app is not directed to children and is not intended for use by anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
